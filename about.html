About Us

Veteran-owned. Client-driven. Built on trust.

We are a veteran-owned and operated company striving to be the best property management and real estate partner in the region.

SEVENAGY LLC brings a different standard to property management and real estate. Our leadership and operational teams apply a military mindset to every project, every client, and every asset under our care.

We started with a simple premise: most property companies talk about service. We measure it. Owners choose us when they want occupancy up, expenses down, and reporting that actually reflects reality.

Discipline

Structured processes, scheduled maintenance, and accountable reporting — not ad-hoc fixes.

Transparency

Owners see the numbers, the issues, and the resolutions without political filtering.

Execution

We don’t just put tenants in units. We improve the asset while we manage it.

Our work is concentrated in the St. Petersburg, Florida area, where we operate local offices and maintain the relationships that keep deals moving and tenants happy.

If you are an owner who values direct communication, predictable results, and a team that treats your property like their own — we should talk.
